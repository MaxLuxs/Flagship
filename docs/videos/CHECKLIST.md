## Technical Requirements

### Video Specifications
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p) minimum
- **Frame Rate**: 30 FPS
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Audio**: Clear narration, no background noise
- **Bitrate**: 5-10 Mbps for good quality

### Audio Requirements
- **Microphone**: Good quality microphone (Blue Yeti, Audio-Technica)
- **Audio Format**: AAC, 128 kbps minimum
- **Noise Reduction**: Use noise reduction in post-processing
- **Volume**: Consistent volume levels

### Recording Setup
- **Screen Recording**: High DPI, clear fonts
- **Cursor**: Visible and smooth
- **Annotations**: Add text overlays for key points
- **Transitions**: Smooth transitions between sections

## Tools

### Recording
- **OBS Studio** (free, cross-platform) - Recommended
- **ScreenFlow** (macOS, paid) - Professional
- **Camtasia** (Windows/Mac, paid) - Easy to use

### Editing
- **DaVinci Resolve** (free, professional) - Recommended
- **Adobe Premiere Pro** (paid) - Industry standard
- **Final Cut Pro** (macOS, paid) - Mac only

### Audio
- **Audacity** (free) - Audio editing
- **Adobe Audition** (paid) - Professional audio

## Thumbnail Guidelines

- **Resolution**: 1280x720 (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **Text**: Large, readable text with video title

- **Branding**: Include Flagship logo
- **Colors**: Use brand colors, high contrast
